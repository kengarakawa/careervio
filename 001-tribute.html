David Bowie, "Starman"

  • 1947 - Born in London, England
  • 1967 - First debut album, "David Bowie", released
  • 1969 - "Space Oddity" released
  • 1972 - "The Rise and Fall of Ziggy Stardust and the Spiders from Mars" released , including "Starman"
  • 1975 - "Young American" released
  • 1976 - First film, "The Man Who Fell to Earth"
  • 1981 - "Under Pressure" collaboration with Queen.
  • 1983 - Film "Merry Christmas, Mr. Lawrence"
  • 1986 - Film "Labyrinth"
  • 2006 - Film "The Prestige"
  • 2016 - Final album, "Blackstar", 2 days after his 69th birthday passed away from liver cancer
